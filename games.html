Tic Tac Toe

Strategy

About This Game

A classic 3x3 grid game where two players take turns marking X and O. The first to get 3 in a row wins!

How to Play:

  1. Player 1 is X, Player 2 is O
  2. Take turns placing your mark on the grid
  3. First to get 3 in a row (horizontal, vertical, or diagonal) wins
  4. If all 9 squares are filled with no winner, it's a draw
