Rock Paper Scissors Tutorial

Difficulty: Easy
01

Basic Rules

Rock Paper Scissors is a simple hand game with three possible moves:

  • Rock beats Scissors
  • Scissors beats Paper
  • Paper beats Rock
02

Game Modes

Three ways to play:

  1. vs AI: Play against computer
  2. Local Multiplayer: Two players on same device
  3. Online Multiplayer: Create or join room with code

Note: First to 5 points wins the match!

03

Multiplayer Features

Local Multiplayer:

  • Player 1 chooses first
  • Player 2 chooses after
  • Choices revealed simultaneously
  • Automatic turn switching

Online Multiplayer:

  • Create room with unique code
  • Share code with friend
  • Both players choose independently
  • Real-time result display
04

Winning Strategies

While Rock Paper Scissors is random, you can try to predict your opponent:

  • Beginners often start with Rock
  • If someone wins with a move, they might repeat it
  • Try to detect patterns in opponent's choices
  • Mix up your choices to be unpredictable
  • Against AI: The computer uses random selection

Tic Tac Toe Tutorial

Difficulty: Medium
01

Game Objective

Be the first to get 3 of your marks in a row on a 3x3 grid:

  • Horizontally (across)
  • Vertically (down)
  • Diagonally (corner to corner)

02

Basic Strategy

  1. Take the center if available (most strategic position)
  2. Take corners if center is taken (second best positions)
  3. Always block your opponent's potential winning moves
  4. Create multiple winning opportunities simultaneously
  5. Watch for forks (positions where you can win in two ways)
03

Game Modes

Two ways to play:

  • vs AI: Play against computer with smart AI
  • 2 Player: Local multiplayer on same device

AI Features:

  • Tries to win when possible
  • Blocks your winning moves
  • Takes strategic positions
  • Adapts to your play style
04

Advanced Tips

  • Control the center to control the game
  • Watch for double threats - positions that create two winning lines
  • Force your opponent to defend instead of attack
  • Practice against different difficulty levels
  • Remember: With perfect play, Tic Tac Toe always ends in a draw!

Memory Match Tutorial

Difficulty: Medium
01

Game Objective

Find all matching pairs of cards in the shortest time with the fewest moves!

  • 16 cards (8 pairs total)
  • Fruit-themed symbols
  • Track your moves and time
  • Challenge yourself to improve
02

How to Play

  1. Click "Start Game" to begin
  2. Click on any card to flip it
  3. Click another card to find its match
  4. If they match, they stay flipped
  5. If not, they flip back after 0.5 seconds
  6. Find all 8 pairs to win!
03

Memory Techniques

  • Create mental associations between card positions
  • Start from corners and work your way in
  • Remember patterns rather than individual cards
  • Take your time - rushing leads to mistakes
  • Practice regularly to improve your memory
04

Scoring System

  • Moves: Each flip counts as one move
  • Time: Timer starts when game begins
  • Goal: Complete with fewest moves in shortest time
  • Challenge: Try to beat your personal best!

Tip: A perfect game can be completed in 16 moves (8 pairs × 2 flips each)

Snake Game Tutorial

Difficulty: Hard
01

Game Objective

Control the snake to eat food and grow as long as possible without dying!

  • Eat food (red circle) to grow longer
  • Avoid walls - hitting them ends the game
  • Don't hit yourself - colliding with your tail ends the game
  • Score points - each food gives 10 points
02

Controls

Desktop Controls:

  • Arrow Keys: Up, Down, Left, Right
  • WASD: Alternative control scheme
  • Spacebar: Pause/Resume game

Mobile Controls:

  • Touch Buttons: On-screen directional buttons
  • Swipe: Swipe in direction to move
  • Tap Pause: Use pause button
03

Game Features

  • High Score System: Best score saved locally
  • Visual Snake: Head with eyes, gradient body
  • Animated Food: Glowing effect for visibility
  • Grid System: 20x20 grid for precise movement
  • Mobile Support: Touch controls and responsive design
  • Pause Function: Spacebar or button to pause game
04

Advanced Strategies

  • Plan your path: Think 2-3 moves ahead
  • Use the walls: Sometimes hugging walls is safer
  • Create space: Keep areas open for movement
  • Speed control: The game gets faster as you grow
  • Practice patterns: Learn efficient movement patterns
  • Stay calm: Panic leads to mistakes!

Tip: Try to keep your snake in a spiral pattern for maximum control!

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to create an account to play?

No! GameHub is completely free and requires no registration. All games work directly in your browser with no login required.

Can I play on mobile devices?

Yes! GameHub is fully responsive and works perfectly on smartphones, tablets, and desktop computers. All games have mobile-friendly controls.

Are the games really free?

Absolutely! All games are 100% free with no hidden costs, ads, or in-app purchases. We believe gaming should be accessible to everyone.

Do games save my progress?

Some games save high scores locally in your browser (like Snake high score), but progress resets if you clear browser data. For persistent saves, we recommend taking screenshots of your achievements!

How does online multiplayer work?

For Rock Paper Scissors: One player creates a room and gets a code, shares it with a friend who enters it to join. Both players make their choices and see results in real-time. No server storage - it's peer-to-peer!

Can I play offline?

Yes! All games work offline once loaded. You can save the webpage and play without internet connection. Perfect for travel or areas with poor connectivity.

Are there keyboard shortcuts?

Yes! Each game has keyboard shortcuts:
- Snake: Arrow keys or WASD, Space to pause
- Tic Tac Toe: Number keys 1-9 for grid positions
- Memory: Number keys for card positions
- RPS: R, P, S keys for quick selection

How can I suggest new games?

We'd love to hear your suggestions! While we don't have a formal submission system yet, you can share ideas through our social media channels or email. We're always looking for new classic games to add!
